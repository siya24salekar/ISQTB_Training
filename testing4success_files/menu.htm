ISEB / ISTQB Foundation in Software Testing Training Course

   

Getting Started

We recommend that you work through each lesson sequentially. Each lesson will include a multiple-choice question along with any key-words you should remember. After completing the training course, attempt each revision quiz until you are regularly scoring around the 80% mark. If there are areas that are not comfortable with, then simply re-visit the specific lesson. Be sure to read the Exam Preparation guide for useful information prior to taking your exam. We wish you the very best of luck with your exam!

Your Tutor

This training course includes four months access to your own personal training tutor. At any point during your studying you can click the 'Ask Tutor' link at the bottom of each page in the training course to ask your course tutor any questions you may have. Our course tutors are certified ISEB Training Instructors and all have a high level of software testing expertise. We ask that you wait for a reply to your question before submitting another question. Your tutor will respond to your question normally between 24 / 48 hours.
